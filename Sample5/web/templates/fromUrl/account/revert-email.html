Revert email address

Use this page to revert your email address back to and reset your password

You should do this in the case where you accidentally changed your email address to an email account that you do not have access to, or where someone else guessed your password and reset the email address on our account to an account that you do not have access to.

Please ensure that you choose a password that is at least 15 characters long and preferably longer. You do not need to use numbers or special characters as this makes very little difference. Ideally your password should be 3 or 4 completely unrelated words that add up to 18 characters or more. This type of password is very hard to crack and very easy to remember.
